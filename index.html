Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the camerakit AR Web Experience (“the App”, “we”, “our”) handles information when you use the web-based augmented reality experience powered by Snap Camera Kit Web SDK.

Last Updated — May 2026

1. Overview

The App is an interactive web experience that allows users to try virtual try-ons and optionally enable a camera-based augmented reality (AR)

We are committed to protecting user privacy and minimizing data collection.

2. Camera Access

If you choose to enable the AR feature:

  • The App will request permission to access your device camera.
  • Camera access is used only to render real-time AR effects.
  • All camera processing occurs locally on your device through the Snap Camera Kit Web SDK.
  • We do not record, store, or upload video or images from your camera to our servers.
  • You can deny or revoke camera permission at any time through your browser settings.

3. Photos & Media

if the App includes a capture feature:

  • Any photo or image generated is saved locally on your device.
  • We do not receive, store, or transmit captured images.
  • We do not maintain a photo database.

4. Information Collection

cameraKit does not collect personally identifiable information.

5. Analytics and Technical Data

We may collect limited anonymous technical data to ensure the experience functions correctly, such as:

  • Browser type
  • Device type
  • General usage events (e.g., page load)
This data does not identify individual users and is used only for performance and stability improvements. If analytics tools are used, they operate in a privacy-respecting, aggregated manner.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses the Snap Camera Kit Web SDK, provided by Snap Inc., to power AR functionality. When the AR feature is active:

  • Camera processing occurs locally on the device.
  • Snap’s SDK may process data required to render lenses and effects.
  • No camera media is stored by us.

8. Children’s Privacy

cameraKit is intended for a general audience and is not specifically directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Your Privacy Choices

We do not create user accounts or store personal data, there is generally no account data to access, correct, or delete.

  • Users may stop using the experience at any time.
  • Users may also control device-level permissions, such as camera access, through the relevant browser or device settings.
  • Please note that camera access is necessary for the real-time experience to function.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to the App or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
